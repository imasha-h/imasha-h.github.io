The Chitra Collection


A showcase of Sri Lankan art that captures the raw beauty and authenticity of the country's culture and traditions.

Our mission is to uncover and promote the untapped potential of Sri Lankan art, and provide a platform for local artists to showcase their work. We believe that by celebrating the beauty and diversity of Sri Lankan art, we can inspire a new generation of artists and contribute to the growth and evolution of this rich cultural heritage.

Our collection features original paintings created by Sri Lankan artists who bring their unique perspective and artistic vision to each piece. From vibrant landscapes to intimate portraits, each work captures the essence of Sri Lanka's rich and diverse culture.

We are committed to supporting emerging talent and encouraging the pursuit of art in Sri Lanka. By providing opportunities for artists to showcase their work, we aim to inspire and cultivate a thriving art community that can continue to evolve and thrive.

Each piece in our collection tells a story and reflects the artist's unique perspective and style. Whether you're an avid collector or simply appreciate the beauty of art, we invite you to explore our collection and discover the magic of Sri Lankan art.
